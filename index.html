Welcome to Pollard's Combe Farm.



          Location

Pollard's Combe farm is 2 miles from Slapton Sands, and provides quality self-catering accomodation set in the tranquil and idylic surroundings of Devon and the edge of the South Devon area of outstanding natural beauty.

Here at Pollard's Combe farm we are proud to be dog friendly and there is a 2 dog maximum limit per cottage. Owners Wayne and Anne are a vet and veterinary nurse respectively and will be happy to provide you with help and advice on your stay.

 



 

Our cottages

Owlacombe Cottage

Our newly refurbished 1 bed romantic getaway.

Decorated to high standards this pretty little cottage provides the perfect escape.

Nestled close to the farmhouse this cottage has its own parking and outside space providing a peacefull and tranquil setting amongst the stunning countryside of South Devon.

The Old Milking Parlour

1 double + 1 twin bedroom. 
 

Attached to one end of the farmhouse, this historic add-on provides the perfect location for a family of 4. With its close proximity to local beaches, devon villages and stunning walks, we are sure this cottage will provide you with you perfect holiday no matter what your prefered activities are.
